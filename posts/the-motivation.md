#### *The beginning*

You are probably wondering what is the purpose of this, and what is the motivation to create this (*I'm certain you are wondering that*). For your luck, this is exactly the reason I'm writing this right now! `:D`

#### *The fast answer*

To give you the fast answer, I just feel like I needed a place to talk about things I like to think about. I'm not really the talking kind of person, and this feels like talking to <del>a wall</del> myself, so it feels easier for me.

#### *The longer fast answer*

There are a lot of things that I want to get off my chest about life, technology, or just share some thoughts that I don't really see happening in personal conversations, so I think these are better suited written on a page that can be accessed by strangers. *What a great idea!*

Besides that, I have been a developer for over 5 years now. I have always wanted to use my tech knowledge to do or create something that I could put my creativity into, something besides solving others' problems. Something truly mine, that doesn't have the obligation to follow the best "industry standards" or be "AI first", simply be able to code and talk about topics that I want to.

Also, in here I can *"chorar minhas pitangas"*[^1] about anything I want without bothering about what people are going to think about, even though I don't really care about it anyway.

#### *Fast notice*

When sharing my thoughts here, I'm not going to use any LLM. I want to be true to my feelings and with you who is going to read it. I already use LLMs a lot on my day-to-day work, more than I wanted to, so I'm going to try to exercise my **HI** (Human Intelligence) when talking to you/myself.

#### *The end*

There is no end here, it's just the beginning of something that I hope is going to be great. See ya! `:P`

[^1]: Brazilian Portuguese expression that roughly translates to "cry over my little cherries" — it means to vent, complain, or pour your heart out about something.
